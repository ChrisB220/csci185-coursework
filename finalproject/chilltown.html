Chilltown

About

Chilltown is a server that I volunteer for and have done so for a couple of years. I help with the development of the updates the server receives. I also help foster its community by gathering people and interacting with those that play.

The development team consists of 2 other developers who have way more experience with running a server and me who started right before the pandemic. It has been a little hobby I've had for a while and I've met a lot of amazing people in that while.
